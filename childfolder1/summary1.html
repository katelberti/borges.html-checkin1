A Summary of The Garden of Forking Paths

The story takes place in World War I. Doctor Yu Tsun is a professor who is in the United Kingdom acting as a spy. He became a spy to prove that Asian men were intelligent to do so. Tsun is on the run and ends up at a house owned by Doctor Albert. Doctor Albert is a fan of Tsun's late relative, Ts'ui Pen's, writing. He was the author of a short story called The Garden of Forking Paths. He tells Tsun that he has studied the work for a long time and discussed with him his theories about the complicated tale. In the end Tsun is pursued by an enemy and is forced to kill Doctor Albert.
